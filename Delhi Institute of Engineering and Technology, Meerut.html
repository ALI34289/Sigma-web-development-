Welcome to Our College

About Us

Delhi Institute of Engineering and Technology, Meerut is a premier engineering college dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in technology and management education. Our campus is known for its diverse academic environment and state-of-the-art facilities.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Engineering (Computer Science, Mechanical, Electrical, Civil)
  • Master of Engineering (Various Specializations)
  • MBA & Other Postgraduate Programs

Admissions

Admissions are open for the academic year. Our rigorous admission process ensures that we attract the best talents who are passionate about learning and making an impact in the world of engineering.

For more information, please check our admissions guidelines or contact our admissions office.

College History & Achievements

Section Details
College History Established in 2005, Delhi Institute of Engineering and Technology, Meerut has a rich legacy of academic excellence and innovation. Our journey began with a vision to provide quality technical education, and over the years we have continuously evolved to meet modern industry standards.
Approved by ACITE Our college is officially approved by ACITE, serving as a testament to our commitment to high educational standards and professional integrity.
Affiliated by AKTU University We are affiliated with AKTU University, ensuring that our curriculum remains relevant and is aligned with the latest academic and research developments.
Research and Documentary Our faculty and students actively engage in research, contributing to significant papers and producing documentaries that highlight our innovative projects and campus life. Explore our research portal for more details.
